Our Compliance Commitments Ensures Quality and Safety
“Our company prides itself in upholding high safety standards and instituting high-functioning management systems for optimal performance. We are committed to producing high-quality, ready-made garments that meet international standards while emphasizing safety, timeliness, and efficiency.”
Ensuring a Secure Work Environment and Encouraging Professional Development.
We are committed to creating a secure workplace environment through a culture that promotes strong safety standards. We aim to provide leadership, influence positive mindsets, and enhance facilities to create a workplace with zero incidents. Furthermore, we work closely with government authorities to ensure that all our employees and subcontractors have the necessary skills and the proper mindset to adopt safe work practices. Finally, our Safety Training Initiative is designed to elevate safety skills for our entire workforce, including suppliers
Investment for Sustainable Safety Culture
State Apparels Ltd. has long prioritized the safety of their workers, ensuring that all factories meet international safety standards and that workers are equipped with all skills needed to work safely. As part of our commitment to furthering our culture of safe work, we have invested in resources, maintenance work, and advanced safety training across all facilities.”
We are committed to:
  • Compliance with this commitment, applicable laws, and all other requirements are the responsibility of every employee, upstream and downstream supplier; as conditioned by their employer or contract.
  • The leadership of State Apparels Ltd. is committed to supporting this policy with all resources and accountability necessary.
  • We will effectively manage the process of designing, building, operating and maintaining our factories to ensure safety, minimize hazards and lower product risks.
  • We are committed to a sustainable and eco-friendly future, and our delivery process ensures that through low carbon emissions and an “environment first” mentality through the product life cycle.
  • Our goal is to ensure zero injuries, occupational illnesses, or workplace incidents.
