Who We Are

"We support our clients in every step of the manufacturing process, from sketch to retail. Our highly skilled team is experienced in creating alongside leading brands around the world and is adept in product development and innovation design."

Message From The Chairman
"State Apparels is a company that embodies what it means to work as a family. Owned and operated by my family, we began our journey in the garments industry from the ground up, in my mother country and second home, Bangladesh. While I was born and raised in New York, I was fortunate enough to have had the pleasure of visiting Bangladesh almost every year and seeing first-hand how the garment industry was the beacon of hope for the future. Bangladesh is a country rich with resources, labor, and a collective determination to build an industrious future-something that has brought the nation to being one of the largest garments exporters in the world.
The key to our business is the people who work to make our process and facility truly great. Our team is fully equipped to provide any essential services required for the completion of an order and is specifically designed to handle any processes at the factory, such as embroidery, print, design, and much more. Ensuring top quality is a must for us as well. Our machines are the latest in their kind of technology, being able to produce clothing at the rate of tens of thousands daily without compromising on quality, consistency, or attention to detail. Combined with our competitive pricing, we present ourselves as the garments manufacturing solution for the future.
As we continue to march forward, we aim to diversify our clientele further and create apparel for brands of all sizes and industries. Our brand is dedicated to the ideas of family, loyalty, and trust; once you work with State Apparels, we will dedicate ourselves to providing excellent customer service, competitive pricing, and unmatched quality. We want our customers to be with us for life, as forming long-term connections is the backbone of our success."
CHAIRMAN
STATE APPARELS LTD
Message From The Managing Director
"In today’s world, creating the perfect blend of quality and design for diverse consumer groups worldwide has become a pressing challenge amongst endless consumer choices and dynamic product offerings. With product pricing more competitive than ever and the weight of industrial sectors pressing on our environment, State Apparels’ focus on sustainable production and manufacturing circularity has become paramount.
These crucial elements are key staples in our business process and supply chain. However, our mission goes beyond simple business metrics— we aim to make a positive impact on society by empowering women and supporting families. By providing a competitive salary, on-the-job training, and funding for children’s basic education, we strive to uplift our neighboring communities.
Our vision is to benefit society through our business, committing our utmost efforts to meet customer needs and ensure sustainable growth. We pledge to use materials from the most sustainable sources, work proactively to conserve energy, and do our utmost to minimize carbon emissions. We are dedicated to creating a sustainable business process that our partners, consumers, and workforce can be proud to be part of. Together, we will build a better future."
MANAGING DIRECTOR
STATE APPARELS LTD
