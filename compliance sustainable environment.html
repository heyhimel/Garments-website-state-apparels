Our Commitment to Sustainability

“State Apparels Ltd. is committed to a green future by putting in practices and systems to ensure a minimized carbon footprint and a positive impact on the environment.”

Promoting Energy Effectiveness Through:
  • Skylights and Solar Panels
  • Solar-Powered Streetlights
  • VRV Inverter Air Conditioning Units
  • Water-Cooled Ventilation System on Production Floors
  • Inverter Type Air Compressors for Machinery
Our Water Conservation Efforts:
  • Harvesting Rainwater for Grass and Plants
  • Push to Start and Sensor Taps in Washrooms and Cafeterias
  • Wastewater Treatment for Use In Toilets and Watering Plants
Reducing Waste By:
  • Composting
  • Recycling
  • Biomass-fired boilers that Operate on Agricultural Waste, Organic Material, and Fabric Waste
“Our commitment to sustainability is one of our top priorities as we continue to develop our plans. Our latest rooftop solar project is one of the many steps we have taken that makes an incredible difference to the surrounding environment and our business..’’
