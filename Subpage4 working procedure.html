Working Procedure

"Our work process is designed to effectively meet your needs, beginning with specific product details, such as fabric composition, measurements, trim, and accessories."

Product & Order details

To effectively meet customer needs, we require specific product details, such as:

  • Fabric Composition -Measurements
  • Trim & Accessories
  • Packaging Specifications
  • Sizing and Quantity Breakdown
  • Preferred Payment Method

Price Calculation

We calculate prices based on product and order specifications. Our decision is based on finding the most optimal solution and comes from negotiation with vendors to provide the best quality at the lowest cost.

Sample Development

After developing the sample according to the specifications, we will send it to the customer for approval. We are open to revisions until we receive approval to move on to the next phase of production. We must receive customer approval of all samples before moving forward, as it allows us to ensure the highest quality and customer satisfaction.

Order Confirmation & Payment

Once the sample is approved, we will proceed with order confirmation through payment. Small quantity orders are recommended to pay through TT or wire transfer, although PayPal is also an acceptable method of transaction. In the case of large orders, we highly recommend using a letter of credit

Production & Quality Check

After order confirmation, we can begin the production process. Quality control is a large part of this process, as we assure that all product specifications are met through rigorous oversight and repeated tests, with only the highest quality garments being approved for the order.

Shipment & Delivery

We can ship orders by air, sea, or courier. The customer can choose to ship with their service or use our shipping- in which case, we deliver directly to the customer's warehouse, deal with customs in their country for clearance, and provide insurance to guarantee safe delivery.
