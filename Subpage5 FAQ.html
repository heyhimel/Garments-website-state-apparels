Frequently Ask Questions

Discover DAQs From our Support

How do i place an order?

You can easily place an order through our website by adding items to your cart and following the checkout proccess.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods to accommodate our clients' preferences. But payment options typically we prefer mostly banking letters of credit (L/C), and that is best secure and convenient methods. We are also open to discussing specific payment arrangements to ensure a seamless transaction process that meets your needs.

Can i get a quote/pricing for a specific product or style?

Of course, you can request a quote for a specific product or style. Please provide us with the details tech pack of the style, including design specifications, size ratio, quantity breakdowns, and any additional requirements of trims & accessories. Our team will promptly prepare a customized quote for your consideration. We are here to accommodate your specific needs and provide competitive & best current market pricing.

Do you provide fit & pp sample before starting production?

Yes, we provide both Fit and Pre-Production (PP) samples before commencing full-scale production. Fit samples ensure that the garment matches your desired specifications and fits as expected. Once approved, Pre-Production (PP) samples are prepared to confirm that the final product aligns with your requirements. These samples are essential steps in ensuring your complete satisfaction with the production process.

How do you ensure quality control during production?

Quality control during production is a top priority for us. We implement robust measures, including random checks, inline, mid & pre final & Final Inspection to maintain high product standards. Our experienced and professional quality control team strictly adheres to procedures and conducts thorough inspections to ensure that every product meets our high-quality standards.

Do you provide regular updates on the production Processes?

Yes, we provide regular updates on the production process. Our merchandising team always updated you on daily basis, ensuring you have visibility into each stage of production. Our commitment to transparency means you'll stay informed about the progress of your order.

Can i visit your production facility & final inspection?

Certainly, you are always welcome to visit our production environment & facility for checking and whatever inspection you want. We value transparency and client involvement as always. If you wish to schedule a visit, please contact our team to make the necessary arrangements & your best supports. You are always most welcome to visit our Office and our Associate Factories.
